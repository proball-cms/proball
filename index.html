Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents need to know before getting started.

ProBall programs start from age 5. Miniball is designed for ages 5–10, and Academy classes are open from age 5 through to 15.

No experience needed and no equipment required. ProBall provides everything for your child's first session. All skill levels are welcome.

Academy and Miniball memberships are flexible — join and leave on your terms. Team memberships run for the full competition season.

Miniball is modified games for ages 5–10 with no scorekeeping or pressure. Academy is structured skills training in small groups for ages 5–15, focused on individual development.

Head to the How to Join page and select your nearest location. New Miniball members get their first 4 weeks free.

Developing the next generation of Australian basketball talent through elite coaching, structured development, and a love for the game.
