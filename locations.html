Our Philosophy

We prioritise long-term player growth over winning alone. Our coaching focuses on smart decision-making, team play, competitive intensity, and pride in representing ProBall — values that extend beyond the court.

Eligibility

Players of all experience levels are welcome. Beginners are encouraged to combine competition with Academy training to build their foundational skills.

Our coaches assess each athlete and place them in the right squad.

How to Get Into Teams

All new players start with a free trial. Coaches assess and place each athlete into the right squad — no prior competition experience needed.
