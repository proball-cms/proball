Ben Osborne

Founder

LinkedIn

Ben has worked in basketball for the last 30 years. A career defined by a love for the game and a commitment to growing it from the ground up. As General Manager of the City of Sydney Basketball Association, Ben built one of the most active grassroots basketball networks in NSW.

A decorated competitor himself, Ben claimed a silver medal at the 2012 London Paralympics — bringing elite performance experience into everything he builds at ProBall. His coaching philosophy is personal, hands-on, and deeply invested in each athlete's long-term growth.

ProBall Founder GM — City of Sydney Basketball 2012 Paralympics Silver Medal

Mike Osborne

Coaching Director

LinkedIn

Mike is one of Australia's early basketball influences — a former NBL coach with the Adelaide 36ers and the West Sydney Weststars, and a founding figure in the sport's development across the country.

Having served as South Australia's State Coaching Director, Mike has shaped the careers of countless players and coaches over decades. His greatest achievement? Founding ProBall in 1982 — a club that has been developing Sydney's young basketball talent ever since.

ProBall Co-Founder (1982) NBL Coach — Adelaide 36ers SA State Coaching Director

Rex Nottage

Technical Adviser

LinkedIn

With over 40 years coaching basketball, Rex Nottage is widely regarded as the GOAT of shooting in NSW. His résumé spans the highest levels of the game — NSW Director of Coaching, NSW ITC Coach, SEABL Coach, NBL Coach, and representative coach for NSW State and Australian national teams.

As Director of Basketball at Newington College, Rex built one of the most decorated school basketball programmes in the country — 624–148 win-loss record, 6 state championships, 10 GPS titles, and top-three finishes in 11 of the last 16 Australian School Championships. Dozens of his players went on to US college programmes.

NCAS Level 3 FIBA Coaching Licence MEd — Coach Education NSW Director of Coaching NBL Coach

Sam Clemens

Operations Manager & Coach

A lifetime basketball junkie obsessed with technical detail. Sam captained Basketball at Newington College in 2019 and co-captained the NSW Basketball team in 2017 — developing the competitive pedigree he now brings to every ProBall session.

Currently studying at the University of Sydney, Sam is driven by a desire to pay forward the guidance he received from his own mentors — helping young athletes grow the same way he did. He keeps operations sharp and the coaching standards high.

Newington College Captain 2019 NSW Basketball Co-Captain 2017 University of Sydney
