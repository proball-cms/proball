ProBall Storms the CSBA Semifinals – 8 Teams Chasing Glory

22 August 2025  ·  ProBall Team

Game day is here.

Eight ProBall teams are competing in the CSBA semifinals — and today is what all of that work has been building towards.

These players have worked, hustled, and pushed themselves to get here. Not just today, not just this week — but across an entire season of early mornings, hard training sessions, and an unwavering commitment to the programme and to each other. The semifinals don't happen by accident. They are the result of months of deliberate, consistent preparation.

To the ProBall Community: Show Up

This is a call to every parent, sibling, friend, and fan in the ProBall family. These teams have given everything to earn their place in these semifinals. Now it's our turn to give something back.

Let's fill the stands, bring the energy, and cheer every play, every bucket, and every defensive stop.

The atmosphere that a crowd creates matters. It lifts players when the game is tight. It steadies nerves when the pressure builds. It reminds every player on the floor that they are part of something bigger than a single game. Be there. Be loud. Be proud of what these kids have achieved.

Event Details

  • Location: CSBA
  • Fixtures: Full draw and game times available at sydneybasketball.com.au
  • Teams competing: Eight ProBall teams across multiple age groups and divisions

Check the fixtures page for your team's specific game time and court assignment. Come early, stay late, and cheer for every ProBall team on the floor today — not just the one your child plays in.

What This Result Reflects

Reaching the CSBA semifinals with eight teams is not a coincidence. It is the direct result of the work that happens in training — week after week, session after session, throughout an entire competition season.

It reflects the early mornings that players chose over sleeping in. The hard sessions they pushed through when their legs were tired and motivation was low. The defensive assignments they committed to. The team moments they bought into. The culture of the ProBall family — players and coaches and parents working together towards something bigger than any individual result.

This is what Built Different looks like in competition.

Congratulations to All Eight Teams

Regardless of today's result — regardless of what happens when the final whistle blows — these players have already achieved something worth celebrating. Reaching a semifinal at any level of competition requires sustained excellence over an extended period. That is not a small thing.

We are proud of every player, every coach, and every family that has contributed to getting these eight teams to this point. The journey matters as much as the destination.

Now go get it.
